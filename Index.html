About ARUX INC

ARUX INC is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of Augmented Reality technology. Our vision is to achieve true AR with glasses that give humans the ability to extend our bodies into the digital world. Our mission is to make AR technology accessible and functional for everyday use.

Current Standing

High-end AR devices are expensive and focus on high fidelity over usability, while low-end devices offer sub-par content and interaction. This prevents mass user adoption and a large marketplace for AR software.

Our Solution

ARUX provides an easily accessible development environment and focuses on usability and functionality over entertainment fidelity. Our goal is to offer a comprehensive AR solution at a competitive price, making AR technology accessible to a wider audience.

Product Offerings

Hardware

  • ARUX Glass: Premium waveguide optics device with bio-monitoring and sleek design for $650.
  • ARUX Compute: Belt-worn compute solution to power ARUX Glass, featuring a hot-swappable battery pack and docking capability for $750.

Software

  • VPS: Obtain meter-accurate GPS coordinates from a single photo, with cross-platform synchronization and a robust software stack.
  • Spatial AI: Intelligent anchoring of XR content, transitioning seamlessly between different environments.
  • ARUX App Store: Main distribution platform for AR applications, offering easy deployment with a 10-20% revenue share.
  • Spatial OS: Intuitive 3D UX for media and OS functions, redefining the web standard for XR browsing.
